# PowerPoint

Use this skill when the user wants a deck, slide outline, or presentation-ready structure.

## Preferred Flow

1. Clarify audience, goal, and deck length.
2. Lock the slide narrative before polishing wording.
3. Keep one slide to one message.
4. Separate headline, evidence, and speaker-note detail.

## Guardrails

- Do not dump essay text into slides.
- Preserve user-provided numbers and claims exactly.
